Welcome to Padi Emas Guild

Padi Emas is an Indonesian guild in free fire games. Padi Emas guild was established since 2018. Until now, Padi Emas guild has touched level 15 with a glory of more than 1000. This makes Padi Emas the largest free fire guild in Indonesia. Hundreds of players have already registered with Padi Emas Guild. Padi Emas Guild houses players who are friendly, willing to learn, and help one another.

Our Logo

Our logo is formed from rice, crowns, and the letter P. Padi symbolizes that the PadiEmas guild can provide welfare for its members. The crown represents leadership, where we want all our members to have the ability to lead in a group. P is the 16th letter of the alphabet, where the PadiEmas Guild was established in 2016.

History

PadiEmas's journey began in 2016, where two students namely Firhan Anshar and Jevi Santosa enjoyed playing games. They have innovations to create a guild that can be followed by many people. In the beginning, very few wanted to join the PadiEmas Guild, and even there were less than ten members. thanks to the hard work of its owner, PadiEmas now has thousands of active users throughout the world.
